Crafting future Signals with AI

IEEE Computer Society and Signal Processing Society College of Engineering Karunagappally presents AITHON - A National Level 24-hour Singal Processing enabled AI Hackathon.The event will be conducted By IEEE Student Branch College of Engineering Karunagappally, Within the 24 hours the participants have to provide a feasible and sustainable solution for the problem statement given related to Artificial Intelligence and Signal Processing. Prior session will be organised to create basic foundations of AI and Singal processing for the students out there, it would be a great opportunity to put in the skills to great astounding and revolutionary solutions. Winners stand a chance to bag exciting prizes and all participants get to walk away with cool goodies.
